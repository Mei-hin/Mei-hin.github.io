Keystone - Multi Purpose Cooperatve Society Limited, bearing Registration Number 1902,
is registered with the Ministry of Cooperatives,since 28th January 2011.
The Society is situated at Royal Road, Petite Riviere.
It operates on an island wide basis.
The KMCS Ltd is a non-profitable organization which is user-owned and user controlled.


Our Mission

The economic and social betterment of all members without gender, social,
political or religious discrimination by means of association based on mutual aid and mutual responsibility.



Our Vision
To encourage and support members to meet their economic and social needs through a democratically controlled association
