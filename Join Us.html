MEMBERSHIP

Membership is opened to all persons, over 18 years of age, of good character and those who indulge in activities
which are conducive to the objectives of the society.

ENTRANCE FEE

On joining KMCS Ltd, the new member shall pay an entrance fee of Rs300 and
purchase a minimum of 100 shares at Rs 10 each.

CONTRIBUTION & SAVINGS

A member must contribute a minimum of Rs 200 every month.

DIVIDENDS

All the financial benefits of the credit union are shared among members as dividends;
after having deducted all expenses.
